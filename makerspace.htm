Barrier potential for Si and Ge are o.6-0.7 and 0.2 to 0.25 V. In LEDs the e hole combination causes light emition which is continuous not depend on nos of e and hole in diode but transferred by battery.

In diode terminal characteristics i = Is [exp(v/VT)-1] here VT = qv/nKT here n is ideality factor and between 1 to 2 , 1 for Ge and 2 for Si . Is for Ge is in order uA and for Si in order nA .
